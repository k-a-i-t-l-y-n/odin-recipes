Cheesecake Recipe

This is a delicious cheesecake recipe from the allrecipes website.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving: 341 calories; 5 protein; carbohydrates 36.2g; fat 20.6g; cholesterol 97.9mg; sodium 277.1mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar, divided
  • 3 (8 oz) packages of cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 medium eggs
  • 1 (21 oz) can cherry pie filling

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix graham crumbs, butter, and 1/4 cup sugar; press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese and remaining sugar in large bowl with mixer until blended. Add sour cream and vanilla; mix well. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed after each addition just until blended. Pour over crust.
  4. Bake 1 hr to 1hr 10 min or until the center is almost set. Run knife around the rim of the pan to loosen cake; cool before removing rim. Refrigerate cheesecake for 4 hrs.
  5. Top with pie filling before serving.
