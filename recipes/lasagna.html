Lasagna Recipe

This is a delicious lasagna recipe from the allrecipes website.

Nutrition Facts

Per serving: 538 calories; 34.8g protein; carbohydrates 36.5g; fat 28.6g; cholesterol 125.7mg; sodium 743.2mg.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (6 oz) cans tomato paste
  • 2 1/4 cups of water
  • 1 pint part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained

Steps

  1. Brown meat with onions and bell peppers, and season to taste. Add tomato paste and water. Let simmer.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mix ricotta cheese with beaten egg. Set aside.
  3. Prehead oven to 370 degrees F. Butter a 9x13 in baking dish.
  4. Begin layering all ingredients beginning with a few spoonfuls of tomato sauce. Follow with noodles, then ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella. Repeat until dish is filled.
  5. Bake at 375 degress F for 35-45 minutes. Let cool a few minutes till serving.
